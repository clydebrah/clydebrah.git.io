2nd Quarter

This quarter, I learned the importance of linking CSS to HTML and creating hyperlinks to connect different websites. Linking CSS to HTML taught me how design and content work together. By linking CSS, you can style your webpage in a way that makes it more appealing and easier to navigate. This process is essential because it separates the structure (HTML) from the design (CSS), making websites more organized and professional-looking.
