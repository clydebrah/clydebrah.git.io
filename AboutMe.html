ABOUT ME

I’m Adrian Clyde Dacoco, 14 years old, and I’m not the type of person who enjoys going to school. Spending 9 to 10 hours in a classroom feels like a waste of time, especially when it’s followed by two more hours of homework. Honestly, I don’t see the point of dedicating so much of my day to something that doesn’t really help me grow in the way I want to.

What I love is lifting weights. When I’m at the gym, I feel like I’m actually doing something meaningful, building strength, discipline, and pushing myself to become better every day. It’s not just about getting stronger physically; it’s about knowing that my time and effort are directly contributing to something tangible. School doesn’t give me that same satisfaction.

To me, sitting through endless classes and assignments feels like my time is being stolen. I could be spending those hours at the gym, focusing on my goals and improving myself. School just feels like a repetitive cycle of tasks that don’t connect to my passions or the future I envision for myself.

I believe that life should be about pursuing what you love, and for me, that’s bodybuilding. Instead of being forced into a routine that drains my energy and wastes my time, I’d rather focus on what truly makes me happy and helps me grow.
